Testimonial

Our Goals

Oak of Joy Women and Children Initiative (OJWOCEIN) coordinates women’s empowerment into poverty reduction, democratic governance, crisis prevention and recovery, and environment and sustainable development.

Through our global network, we work to ensure that women have a real voice in all governance institutions, from the judiciary to the civil service, as well as in the private sector and civil society, so they can participate equally with men in public dialogue and decision-making and influence the decisions that will determine the future of their families and countries. While it is imperative that women smile, Less privileged children will not be left out.

Who we are

Oak of Joy Women and Children Empowerment Initiative (OJWOCEIN) is an indigenous non-governmental and non-profit organization in Nigeria. Oak of Joy was founded in the year 2011 by Rev. Mrs. Victoria U. Ejim,

I am motivated by the desire to make a difference in the lives of the people, especially the poor and less privileged. I see a lot of people in need around me and I see a need to put a smile on their faces. I also experienced a form of deprivation while growing up. So I know what it is to go without food for days, or live without those basic needs of life.

OJWOCEIN provides educational, nutritional, skills acquisition/economic empowerment; and psycho- social supports for women and children irrespective of their tribe, state of origin or location in Nigeria.

Newsflash: Educational Empowerment

From 2013 – 2016 Academic Year, Oak of Joy Women and Children Empowerment Initiative (OJWOCEIN) has sponsored eighty one (81) indigent students at various educational levels- Nursery to tertiary. The cost of this endeavour stands at Two Million, ninety six thousand and sixty naira only (N2,096,060.00). Four of our undergraduates are now graduates and four are still in school.

We still have many pupils in the primary schools and students in the secondary school. Economic empowerment From 2014 till date, we have empowered seven (7) indigent women mostly widows to various forms of small scale businesses. It cost us One Million, Two hundred and forty nine thousand naira only(N1,249,000.00). the various amounts have been from my personal contributions and from the proceeds of my 60th birthday which was dedicated wholly to this cause.

Urgent Cause

Oak of Joy Women and Children Initiative (OJWOCEIN) is a financially stable organization. We hold 6 months of operating funds in reserve, and our overhead is low: 18.9%. That means for every naira you donate to Oak of Joy Women and Children Initiative (OJWOCEIN), less than 19 kobo covers administrative costs.

Oak of Joy Women and Children Initiative (OJWOCEIN) is a certified Assemblies of God Church/GABASAWA Women and Children Empowerment Initiative partners. We neither solicit, nor recieve, goverment funding. You won’t find any frills here: our computers, office furniture, and even a portion of our rent are donated.

Our priority is the homeless women and children we serve and our spending reflects our commitment.
