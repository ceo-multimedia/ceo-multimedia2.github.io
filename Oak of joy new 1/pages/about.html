About us

Oak of Joy Women and Children Empowerment Initiative (OJWOCEIN) is an indigenous non-governmental and non-profit organization in Nigeria. Oak of Joy was founded in the year 2011 by Rev. Mrs. Victoria U. Ejim.

I am motivated by the desire to make a difference in the lives of the people, especially the poor and less privileged. I see a lot of people in need around me and I see a need to put a smile on their faces. I also experienced a form of deprivation while growing up. So I know what it is to go without food for days, or live without those basic needs of life.

OJWOCEIN provides educational, nutritional, skills acquisition/economic empowerment; and psycho- social supports for women and children irrespective of their tribe, state of origin or location in Nigeria.
